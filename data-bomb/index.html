7 ways I learned
how not to be a data bomb.
Here we go!

#1:

Take notes.
Extensive notes.

To-do lists, annotations on data, steps for cleaning

#2:

Spot check.
Constantly.

To assume makes...

#3:

Automate if possible.

Your human-ness can ruin everything.

#4:

Comment your code and your files.

Dates, descriptions, the whole shebang

#5:

Refrain from randomly clicking around.

#6:

Be patient.

Go slow as mud if you have to.
 Keep your eye on the numbers.

#7:

Always question
your data.

And strive to answer  those questions.
Even/especially when you feel really confident about it.

Thanks for listening!
